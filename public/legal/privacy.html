Section 2

Data We Collect

Current (Pilot Phase)

  • Name — to identify you within your circle
  • Email address — for account communications and notifications
  • Phone number — for borrowing coordination within circles
  • Photos of items — uploaded by you to create item listings
  • Transaction data — borrow requests, approvals, return records within circles
  • AI-estimated retail value (price) — an AI-generated estimate of an item's retail value, shown to the lender only. Used internally for economic impact reporting. Private by default. If the lender enables the visibility toggle, it becomes visible to all members of that circle — visibility cannot be limited to specific members.
  • AI-estimated item weight — an AI-generated estimate used internally for environmental impact calculations (CO₂ savings). Private by default. If the lender enables the visibility toggle, it becomes visible to all members of that circle — visibility cannot be limited to specific members.
  • Usage data — how you interact with the Platform (pages visited, features used) to improve the service

⚙️ Visibility controls: Retail price and item weight are private by default. A single visibility toggle on the listing form controls both values together. When enabled, both the estimated price and weight become visible to all members of your circle — not selectively to individual members. This is entirely optional and can be changed at any time by editing your listing.

Future Versions (Not Collected Yet)

  • Delivery address — when logistics/shipping features are introduced
  • Videos of items — for enhanced listings
  • Payment information — processed exclusively by third-party payment processors; CIN will not store card numbers or bank details
  • Deposit information — held in escrow by third-party processors, not by CIN

We practice data minimization: we collect only what is necessary for the Platform to function. We will update this policy before introducing any new categories of data collection.

Section 3

How We Use Your Data

  • Operating the Platform — enabling you to create an account, join circles, list items, and complete borrowing transactions
  • Communications — sending borrow requests, confirmations, reminders, return notifications, and support responses
  • Circle functionality — sharing your name and contact details with other members of circles you join, so transactions can be coordinated
  • Safety and trust — maintaining transaction records to support dispute resolution between users
  • Platform improvement — understanding how the Platform is used so we can fix bugs and develop better features
  • Mission reporting — generating aggregate, anonymized impact metrics (items shared, estimated waste avoided) for CIN's nonprofit reporting and mission
  • Internal impact calculations — AI-estimated retail values and item weights are used solely to calculate environmental and economic impact metrics (e.g., estimated CO₂ savings, estimated value kept in circulation). These values are never used for pricing, advertising, or commercial purposes, and are never shared with other users without the lender's explicit consent via the visibility toggle
  • Legal compliance — meeting our obligations under applicable laws

We do not use your data for targeted advertising, sell your data to third parties, or use it for any purpose unrelated to ShareCircle and CIN's charitable mission. AI-estimated price and weight values are always private by default — visible to your full circle only if you choose to enable the visibility toggle.

Section 4

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / UK GDPR)

For users in the EU, EEA, and UK, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) — processing necessary to provide the Platform services you've signed up for (account creation, circle participation, borrowing transactions)
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — improving the Platform, maintaining security, and aggregate impact measurement, where these interests are not overridden by your rights
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — for any optional features or communications beyond core Platform operation; you may withdraw consent at any time
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) — where processing is required by applicable law
Section 5

Data Sharing

With Other Circle Members

When you join a circle, your name and contact details are visible to other members of that circle so that lending and borrowing transactions can be coordinated. You control which circles you join.

With Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who help operate the Platform (including hosting, email delivery, and analytics). These providers are contractually required to process data only on our instructions and to maintain appropriate security. Where required by law (including GDPR Article 28), we maintain Data Processing Agreements with these providers.

We do not publicly name specific service providers in this Policy, as relationships may change over time. A current list is available upon request at privacy@circularimpact.org.

Service provider security: While we select providers carefully and require contractual security commitments, CIN is not liable for security lapses, breaches, or failures on the part of third-party providers that are outside our direct control. Should a provider breach affect your data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

With Payment Processors (Future)

When payment or deposit features are introduced, transactions will be handled by third-party payment processors. Those processors will handle payment data under their own privacy policies. CIN will not store or have access to payment card numbers or bank account details.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal data to any third party
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers
  • We do not use your data for behavioral advertising

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of CIN, its users, or the public.

Section 6

International Data Transfers

CIN is based in the United States. Your personal data is processed and stored in the United States, which may not have data protection laws equivalent to those in your country.

EU / EEA / UK Users

We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission as the transfer mechanism for personal data transferred from the EU, EEA, or UK to the United States. By using ShareCircle, you acknowledge this transfer.

Other International Users

By using the Platform from outside the United States, you acknowledge and consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States.

Section 7

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained for as long as your account is active
  • Closed accounts: Data deleted or anonymized within 90 days of account deletion, except where retention is required by law
  • Transaction records: Retained for up to 3 years after the transaction to support dispute resolution and legal compliance
  • Aggregate anonymized data: May be retained indefinitely for mission reporting; this data cannot identify you
  • Legal holds: Data may be retained longer if required by applicable law or legal proceedings

Pilot and Beta Phase Data

Data collected during the ShareCircle pilot and beta phase will be deleted at the conclusion of the pilot, unless a circle explicitly requests that its data be retained for continuity. Circle leads wishing to retain data beyond the pilot should contact support@circularimpact.org before the pilot concludes.

To request early deletion of your data, see Section 8 (Your Rights).

Section 9

Children's Privacy

Age Requirement

ShareCircle requires users to be at least 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

COPPA (United States — Under 13)

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents/guardians who believe we have collected their child's data should contact us at support@circularimpact.org immediately.

Teens (13–17)

We encourage users aged 13–17 to use the Platform with parental awareness. We do not engage in behavioral advertising, cross-site tracking, or profiling of any users, which is especially important for minors.

India (DPDPA — Under 18)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, users under 18 should obtain verifiable parental consent before using ShareCircle. We do not knowingly track, profile, or target advertising to users under 18.

EU/EEA (GDPR Article 8)

Age of consent varies by EU member state (13–16). Users in those jurisdictions should ensure compliance with their local age threshold. We recommend parental involvement for all users under 16 accessing the Platform from the EU.

Section 10

Security

CIN implements reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These include encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), access controls, and secure development practices.

No system is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account password and for notifying us promptly at support@circularimpact.org if you suspect unauthorized access.

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant supervisory authorities as required by applicable law: within 72 hours under GDPR (for breaches posing risk to users), and in accordance with US state breach notification laws and India's DPDPA requirements.

Section 11

Jurisdiction-Specific Details

🇺🇸 United States

Data is processed in the United States and governed primarily by U.S. federal and state laws. As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, CIN is exempt from CCPA/CPRA (which applies only to for-profit businesses). We voluntarily provide access, correction, and deletion rights to all U.S. users. All commercial emails comply with CAN-SPAM, including a clear opt-out mechanism.

🇪🇺 European Union / 🇩🇪 Germany (GDPR)

CIN processes personal data of EU/EEA residents in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Legal bases are set out in Section 4. EU users may lodge complaints with their national data protection authority (see edpb.europa.eu). International transfers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses.

🇮🇳 India (DPDPA 2023)

CIN acknowledges that India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 applies extraterritorially to platforms serving Indian users. Indian users have rights of access, correction, erasure, grievance redressal, and the right to nominate a representative. We rely on user consent as the legal basis for processing personal data of Indian users. We do not track, profile, or target children under 18 in India. We will comply with DPDPA rules as they are issued by the Indian government.

🇨🇦 Canada (PIPEDA + Quebec Law 25)

Canadian users' data is handled in accordance with the federal Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). Quebec users additionally benefit from Law 25 (Law 64) protections, including rights of data portability and information about automated decision-making. We voluntarily provide access and correction rights to all Canadian users. Contact us at privacy@circularimpact.org for privacy requests.

🇬🇧 United Kingdom (UK GDPR)

UK users are protected under the UK GDPR, which mirrors EU GDPR protections and is administered by the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). UK users have the same rights as EU users described in Section 8. Complaints may be submitted to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

🇦🇺 Australia (Privacy Act 1988)

CIN likely falls below the AUD $3 million threshold triggering mandatory Privacy Act compliance at pilot scale. However, we voluntarily honor Australia's Information Privacy Principles, including rights of access and correction for Australian users.

🇧🇷 Brazil (LGPD)

Brazilian users have rights under the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD), including access, correction, deletion, portability, and the right to revoke consent. We rely on user consent as our legal basis for processing personal data of Brazilian users. To exercise LGPD rights, contact privacy@circularimpact.org.

🇳🇿 New Zealand (Privacy Act 2020)

We voluntarily honor New Zealand's Information Privacy Principles, including access and correction rights for New Zealand users.

🌍 All Other Countries

If you are located in a country not listed above, your use of ShareCircle is subject to this Privacy Policy and U.S. law. We recognize that local laws may grant you additional rights. We are committed to honoring reasonable privacy requests from users anywhere in the world. To the extent that mandatory provisions of your local law conflict with this Policy, those mandatory provisions apply. Contact us at privacy@circularimpact.org with any privacy request.
